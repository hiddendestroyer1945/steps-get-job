# Steps to Get a Job
* **Description**:  To explain about the, actions for get a job and  steps to get a job. 

* **Actions for Get a Job**:    Here, we explain what activities a job seeker needs to take to get a job in their chosen field.
  * **Decide Specific Job**:    According to the activities in the profile roadmap, the job seeker should acquire the necessary training and knowledge to get a job in the chosen field, build a resume accordingly, and search only for the specific job decided upon in the activities in the profile roadmap.
  * **Effective Self-Introduction**:    After acquiring the necessary training and knowledge to get a job in the chosen field according to the activities in the profile roadmap, the job seeker should be able to build a resume accordingly and confidently present the information in the About Me section of the profile roadmap to the interview committee in a few words.
  * **Know Your Value**:    After the job seeker has acquired the necessary training and knowledge to get a job in the chosen field according to the activities in the profile roadmap, and after building a resume accordingly, the job seeker should be asked in front of the interview committee exactly how much income the job seeker needs. If this is not asked, the organization will assume that the job seeker is not confident in the job seeker's abilities. But, it is always better to ask for more than necessary because there is a possibility of bargaining.
  * **Open Meterial Education is Important**:    According to the activities in the profile roadmap, after the job seeker has acquired the necessary training and knowledge to get a job in his chosen field, after building a resume accordingly, and after being able to explain to the interview committee that he has acquired the knowledge and training using the open material education method, the organization will understand that the job seeker is passionate about his chosen field and is able to find and fix mistakes on his own if they occur. Therefore, the organization will value this more than certificates.
  * **Showcase Your Skillset**:   According to the activities in the profile roadmap, after the job seeker has acquired the necessary training and knowledge to get a job in the chosen field, and after building a resume accordingly, the job seeker should be able to present the training and knowledge acquired in the chosen field to the interview committee through this resume. For this, the job seeker's resume should be different from the resume of others. For this, the job seeker should also acquire other knowledge other than the knowledge required to get a job in the chosen field, and the job seeker should understand the organization he is approaching for the chosen job and acquire additional skills required by that organization. 
  * **Create a Linkedin Profile**:   According to the activities in the profile roadmap, after the job seeker has acquired the necessary training and knowledge to get a job in the field of his choice, and after building a resume accordingly, if the link to the job seeker's LinkedIn profile appears in the resume submitted by the job seeker to get a job in the field of his choice, all firms that do not conduct social proof and professional validation will use this LinkedIn profile to collect information about the job seeker.
  * **Create a Personal Website**:   After the job seeker has acquired the necessary training and knowledge to get a job in the chosen field according to the activities in the profile roadmap, and after building a resume accordingly, the resume submitted by the job seeker to get a job in the chosen field should include a link to the website job seeker has built himself/herself. This website should include information about the knowledge and training acquired according to the activities in the profile roadmap for the chosen field, his/her own discoveries, and information about the projects, contributions, part-time work, and marketing done according to the activities in the profile roadmap.
  * **Apply for Jobs**:   According to the activities in the profile roadmap, after the job seeker acquires the necessary training and knowledge to get a job in the chosen field and builds a resume accordingly, the job seeker creates a profile on job advertising sites such as LinkedIn, Naukri, Indeed, and Upwork and uses the job opportunities available to this profile or searches for and finds the required job opportunities on these sites. In addition, the job seeker can get a job by collecting information about firms from the apollo.io and hunter.io and even google maps for local firms on the Internet and sending the resume to the email of those firms, or by contacting the firms whose information was collected from the Yellow Pages directories by phone.

* **Steps to Get a Job**:    Here is an explanation of the steps a job seeker must go through to get a job in their chosen field.
  * **Step-1: Telephonic Interview**:    It explains what to do when attending a telephonic interview to get a job in the chosen field after the job seeker has acquired the necessary training and knowledge to get a job in the chosen field according to the activities in the profile roadmap, and after building a resume accordingly.
    * **Introduce Yourself**:    After the job seeker has acquired the necessary training and knowledge to get a job in the chosen field according to the activities in the profile roadmap, and has built a resume accordingly, when attending a telephonic interview to get a job in the chosen field, the information in the About Me section of the activities in the profile roadmap is presented with great confidence and without drama.
    * **Ask for Salary**:    After the job seeker has acquired the necessary training and knowledge to get a job in the chosen field according to the activities in the profile roadmap, and has built a resume accordingly, when participating in a telephonic interview to get a job in the chosen field, the job seeker should ask the organization exactly how much income the job seeker needs. But, it is always better to ask for more than necessary because there is a possibility of bargaining.
    * **It is Negotiable**:    After the job seeker has acquired the necessary training and knowledge to obtain a job in the chosen field according to the activities in the profile roadmap, and has built a resume accordingly, when participating in a telephonic interview to obtain a job in the chosen field, the job seeker should only give in to a small amount of negotiation to the organization's demand that the salary be negotiable, however, the company should not accept everything it says, otherwise it will cause the organization to doubt the skills described in the job seeker's resume. 
  * **Step-2: Face to Face Interview**:    It explains what to do when attending a face-to-face interview to get a job in the chosen field after the job seeker has acquired the necessary training and knowledge to get a job in the chosen field according to the activities in the profile roadmap, and after building a resume accordingly.
    * **They Will Ask Basics**:    After the job seeker has acquired the necessary training and knowledge to get a job in the chosen field according to the activities in the profile roadmap, and after building a resume accordingly, when participating in a face-to-face interview to get a job in the chosen field, the interview board will ask the job seeker to explain the basic knowledge in the chosen field, the basic knowledge in the sub-fields of the chosen field, the basic knowledge of the tasks to be done in the chosen job, the projects described in the resume, the website built by the job seeker and the technologies used in them. Failure to do so will cause the organization to distrust all the statements made in the resume. 
    * **Introduce Yourself**:    After the job seeker has acquired the necessary training and knowledge to get a job in the chosen field according to the activities in the profile roadmap, and has built a resume accordingly, when attending a face-to-face interview to get a job in the chosen field, the information in the About Me section of the activities in the profile roadmap is presented with great confidence and without drama. 
  * **Step-3: Machine Test**:    It explains what to do when attending a machine test to get a job in the chosen field after the job seeker has acquired the necessary training and knowledge to get a job in the chosen field according to the activities in the profile roadmap, and after building a resume accordingly. This can be done by visiting the institution in person, remotely accessing the institution's temporary network, or by sending the institution a project and its GitHub link.
    * **Keyboard Typing Style**:    After the job seeker has acquired the necessary training and knowledge to get a job in the chosen field as per the activities in the profile roadmap, and has built a resume accordingly, when participating in the machine test to get a job in the chosen field, if the job seeker attends this machine test in person at the institution, the job seeker should be able to do the touch typing method at an average speed. Because lack of typing skills can lead to a decrease in the reputation of the job seeker.
    * **Coding Standards**:    After the job seeker has acquired the necessary training and knowledge to get a job in the chosen field according to the activities in the profile roadmap, and after building a resume accordingly, when participating in the machine test to get a job in the chosen field, if there are any coding-related things in this machine test, they must be done according to the coding standards. Because ignorance of the coding standards can cause the organization to doubt the job seeker's knowledge.
    * **Cyber Security Report**:    After the job seeker has acquired the necessary training and knowledge to get a job in the chosen field as per the activities in the profile roadmap, and has built a resume accordingly, when participating in the machine test to get a job in the chosen field, if any cybersecurity checks are performed in this machine test, this cybersecurity review should be submitted to the organization as a cybersecurity report. This will increase the reputation of the job seeker in the organization.
    * **Push the Link**:    After the job seeker has acquired the necessary training and knowledge to get a job in the chosen field according to the activities in the profile roadmap, and has built a resume accordingly, when participating in the machine test to get a job in the chosen field, the job seeker completes the tasks required by the firm in this machine test, pushes them to GitHub for sending to the firm, and sends the link to that GitHub repository to the firm.
    * **Update the Work Progress**: After the job seeker has acquired the necessary training and knowledge to get a job in the chosen field according to the activities in the profile roadmap, and has created a resume accordingly, when participating in the machine test to get a job in the chosen field, if the job seeker follows the pattern of completing the tasks required by the organization in this machine test and sending them to the organization. If these tasks last for several days, a status report of the tasks completed each day should be prepared and sent to the organization.
  * **Step-4: High Level Discussion**:    After the job seeker has acquired the necessary training and knowledge to get a job in the chosen field according to the activities in the profile roadmap, and after creating a resume accordingly, the organization tests the job seeker's communication skills, ability to present ideas, and ability to express opinions openly when participating in a high-level discourse to get a job in the chosen field. In this, the information in the About Me section of the job seeker's profile roadmap, the job seeker's skills, and the income required by the job seeker are subject to the discourse.
